# investment-bot

## Vision
Build an intelligent, AI-driven investment platform that operates across crypto markets, stock exchanges, business investing, and decentralized asset management.

## Mission statement
To deliver a disciplined, data-driven investment engine that identifies high-value opportunities, executes risk-aware strategies, and continuously optimizes portfolio performance across centralized and decentralized markets.

## Value proposition
- Multi-market intelligence in one platform (crypto, equities, business, and DeFi).
- Real-time decisioning powered by analytics and machine learning.
- Adaptive risk controls that enforce exposure and downside limits.
- Portfolio optimization through strategy selection and rebalancing logic.
- Unified automation that reduces manual, fragmented workflows.

## Problem / solution framing
### Problem
Investors face fragmented tooling, noisy multi-market data, fast regime changes, and inconsistent decision-making under volatility.

### Solution
This platform ingests cross-market data, evaluates opportunities with specialized AI agents, and executes disciplined strategies under a strict risk engine and auditable workflows.

## Core development pipeline (V1 -> V2)
1. **Data ingestion & storage**: collect market, on-chain, and sentiment data in normalized, queryable storage.
2. **Backtesting engine**: validate strategies with historical simulation, slippage assumptions, and risk scenarios.
3. **Strategy + risk rules**: apply position sizing, exposure limits, stop-loss/take-profit, and allocation constraints.
4. **Paper trading**: verify execution, latency, data integrity, and rule enforcement without capital risk.
5. **Controlled live trading**: deploy with minimal capital for real-world validation.
6. **Advanced AI agents**: add regime detection, sentiment scoring, and portfolio intelligence after core stability.

## System capability overview
- **Market Regime Agent**: classifies trend/chop/volatility regimes and adjusts strategy mix.
- **Crypto Trading Agent**: handles 24/7 crypto-specific signals and execution contexts.
- **Equity Trading Agent**: incorporates equity market structure, macro, and fundamental signals.
- **Business Investment Agent**: scores companies/projects using financial and narrative analysis.
- **Risk & Compliance Agent**: enforces hard risk limits, auditability, and emergency controls.

## Expansion roadmap (V3+)
- On-chain vaults for transparent, programmable asset management.
- DAO governance for strategy/risk parameter voting.
- Multi-user asset management with deposits, risk profiles, and performance dashboards.

## Founder narrative (investor-ready)
We are building a next-generation investment operating system that combines AI-native market intelligence with strict risk discipline. Our differentiators are a specialized multi-agent architecture, auditable decision trails across every trade, and a phased deployment model (backtest -> paper -> controlled live) that prioritizes capital protection while scaling. By unifying crypto, equities, business analysis, and decentralized finance in one modular architecture, the platform is designed to scale from a focused trading engine into a transparent multi-user investment ecosystem.
